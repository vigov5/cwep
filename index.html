About Chatwork Emoticons Plus

Chatwork Emoticons Plus is a free Chrome Extension which provides more funny emoticons for Chatwork. It is made by Framgia's Members, and for Framgia's Members.

Download Chatwork Emoticons Plus

You can download Chatwork Emoticons Plus for free with the link bellow !

■　After downloading, go to chrome://extensions/ , and then drag the CWEP extension to that page to install
■　To update extension, go to chrome://extensions/ page, turn on Developer mode and click "Update extensions now"
